Frequently Asked Questions

What material do you use?

We use high quality yarns and cotton depending on your design needs.

Is there a physical store where i can try items on?

Currently, we only operate online. This allows us to focus on providing the best online shopping experience. However, we are planning to open a physical store in the near future.

Can i cancel my order?

Cancellations are accepted within 24-hours of placing an order. Once we recieve your cancellation request, we will process your refund. After 24 hours, cancellations are not accepted.

Do you offer custom designs?

Yes, we offer custom design services to create unique pieces that reflect your personal style. Please contact us to provide any relevant images or descriptions.

What is your return policy?

Due to items being made to order, we usually do not accept returns. However if there is a flaw in your product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery so that we can resolve the issue.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept EFT payments and secure online payments. Your order can only be confirmed once the payment is recieved.

How do i take care of my crotchet pieces?

To keep your items in a perfect condition, we recommend washing in cold water. Do not hand them as this might stretch the yarn.

Are your items see through?

When crocheting we use tight stitching patterns to ensure that our items are not see through. However, for certain designs, we recommend wearing something underneath.
